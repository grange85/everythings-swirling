I took Adam to see Gordon from Ballboy on Friday night - unfortunately the two support acts went on a bit too long and by the time Gordon hit the stage (well the floor at the front of the room) Adam was barely awake and looking

forward to going home...

It was a good show for all that. Started with "A Day in Space" which was not only great - but a song that Adam could relate to (he's going through a bit of a space phase at the moment). And a song about cannibals falling in love and having to deal with the double-edged sword of desire/delectation was also amusing.

I think I enjoyed it more than Adam but he did recognise songs when I played them to him ("he did this one didn't he?").
