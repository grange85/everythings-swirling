Imported from my last.fm journal 2012-03-20

The Clientele are a four piece now, having added (and placed centre stage) a violin/keyboard/percussion player.

The set was a mix of classics ((I Want You) More Than Ever and Since K Got Over Me) along with a few tracks from their next album God Save The Clientele (out in April) and a couple of covers (A Picture Of Dorian Grey and The Fire).

The chatty crowd were a little annoying - soliciting a "Shut the fuck up" from Alasdair which only partly worked but all told another good Clientele gig.
