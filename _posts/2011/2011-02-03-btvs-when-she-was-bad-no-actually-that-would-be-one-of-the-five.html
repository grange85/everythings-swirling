So... I wanted to pick up on the pop in Buffy when it came to pop I liked or noticed, or by bands I knew - but Cibo Matto's contributions to When She Was Bad were a little unimpressive. I bought Hazel the Miho Hatori solo album Ecdysis on the strength of the Buffy connection - and certainly like it more than the Cibo Matto songs in this episode.

Instead... I'll just quote one of my all time fave bits of Buffy dialogue (there are so many bits of dialogue in that list!)

Snyder: There're some things I can just smell. It's like a sixth sense.
Giles: No, actually that would be one of the five.
