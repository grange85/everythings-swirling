There is, of course, a WordPress plug-in for everything and so it was negligent of me not to consider that I could have my cake AND eat it. Through the power of the lovely FeedWordPress I can have all those music posts I make on my Last.fm journal appear, as if by magic, on this blog. OK it does mean the same thing being in two places but it is still a good thing!
