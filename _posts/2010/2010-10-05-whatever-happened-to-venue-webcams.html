I remember watching videos of shows on crappy webcams 10 years ago - but that doesn't seem to happen anymore... which is a shame. The Knitting Factory in New York had a webcam and I mentioned to Dean in an email in 2001 that I'd be getting up early to watch Luna's New Year's Eve show. That was (to my knowledge) the only time I ever got name-checked from the stage at a Luna show... unfortunately the webcam was broken and I crawled back to bed and only (and barely) heard my name check on a recording of the show months later...

And now you can too... here's the recording of the last song from Luna's 2001 new year's show - it's California (All The Way) and if you listen very carefully to the intro you might, just hear my one Luna name-check...


Luna @ The Knitting Factory 2001-12-31 (late show)
