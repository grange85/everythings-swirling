So it only took to day 4 before Hazel and I agreed on one. Favourite female character on Buffy is of course Willow because...
  • She's cute
  • She's clever
  • She's great at looking things up on the Internet
  • She has great hair
  • She likes libraries and books
  • and (as Hazel points out) she picks great partners
All those characteristics are very obviously attractive to me.

So ... Favourite Female Character = Willow
