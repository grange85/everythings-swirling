Oh Hazel thinks she's so very smart, she thinks she knows me so well - "it'll be Glory" she says... and it is.

It shouldn't be - it should be Drusilla because of "Grrr shhh bad dog" but it is Glory because...

She was almost as funny as Dru, particularly when fighting Buffybot or having been teleported into the air; she had particularly great minions; she was part of the rather good S5 story arc; she gave all the scoobies a beating and looked great doing it - which makes Hazel's claim that "Clare Kramer was too nice to make her properly villainous" a nonsense.

So favourite villain is very obviously Her Splendiferousness
