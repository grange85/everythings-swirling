The Clientele
Originally uploaded by grange85.

Ahhh The Clientele - tremendous show played in near darkness (my excuse for the rubbish pics I took - although clearly not a very good excuse). They played lots from the very lovely new album Strange Geometry and were joined by Lupe from support act Pipas for a number.

Pipas had earlier entertained the crowd with lovely twee songs and insanely loud and unwanted burps and farts from the technology.

You can listen to some Clientele new and old on their Myspace space.
