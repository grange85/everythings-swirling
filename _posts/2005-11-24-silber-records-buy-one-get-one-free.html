Now I have to admit that I have downloaded and enjoyed a number of Aarktica tracks over the last few years but, bad boy that I am, I never got around to actually buying anything. Now Silber Records are doing a fab Buy one get one free deal at their online Paypal driven site and I no longer have an excuse.

Plenty to choose from and the site has stacks of stuff to download so you can try before you buy (including complete albums hosted by the Internet Archive). Have a poke around and you'll find some tracks by the very excellent Rivulets.
